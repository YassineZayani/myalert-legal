Privacy Policy

This policy explains how MyAlert collects, uses, and protects your data.

1. Who We Are

MyAlert is an email alert application developed by Yassine Zayani ("we", "our", "us"). The app monitors your connected email inboxes and sends you real-time notifications based on rules you create.

Contact: contact.myalert@gmail.com

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the service:

  • Email address — used to identify your account and connect your mailbox.
  • OAuth tokens — access tokens issued by Google, Microsoft, or Yahoo to read your inbox on your behalf. Stored encrypted on our server.
  • Email metadata and content — sender address, subject line, and email body are read to evaluate your alert rules. We do not store email content permanently; it is processed in memory and immediately discarded.
  • Alert history — a log of triggered alerts (sender, subject, date/time) stored locally on your device.
  • Push notification token — the device token issued by Apple to deliver notifications to your iPhone.
  • App settings and rules — your alert rules, categories, and preferences, stored on our server linked to your account.
  • Purchase status — whether you hold an active MyAlert Pro subscription, managed by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your payment details.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To connect to your mailbox and monitor incoming emails in real time.
  • To evaluate your alert rules and send push notifications when a match is found.
  • To synchronize your rules and settings across sessions.
  • To restore your account state when you reinstall or upgrade the app.
  • To respond to support requests you initiate.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the MyAlert service.

4. Google User Data

When you connect a Gmail account, MyAlert requests access via OAuth 2.0 to read your inbox. Specifically:

Scope requested: https://mail.google.com/
This grants MyAlert the ability to read your Gmail messages via IMAP in order to detect emails that match your alert rules.

MyAlert's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

  • We only read emails to evaluate your personal alert rules.
  • We do not share Google user data with any third party except as described in Section 5.
  • We do not use Google user data for advertising or AI/ML model training.
  • Email content is processed in memory and never persisted on our servers.
  • You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account permissions page.

5. Third-Party Services

MyAlert integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth 2.0 — authentication and inbox access for Gmail accounts. Google Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Identity Platform — authentication for Outlook/Hotmail accounts. Microsoft Privacy Policy
  • Yahoo OAuth 2.0 — authentication for Yahoo Mail accounts. Yahoo Privacy Policy
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) — delivery of real-time notifications to your device.
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — subscription management for MyAlert Pro.
  • RevenueCat — subscription status tracking. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Render (onrender.com) — cloud hosting for our backend server, where your tokens and rules are stored securely.
  • Expo / React Native — the framework used to build the app.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers hosted by Render (United States). We apply the following security measures:

  • OAuth tokens are stored encrypted.
  • All communications between the app and our server use HTTPS/TLS.
  • We do not log or store the content of your emails.
  • Alert history is stored locally on your device only.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account or reset the app, all associated data (rules, tokens, settings) is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to restriction — request that we limit processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object — object to processing of your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact.myalert@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy

MyAlert is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
